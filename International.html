US dismisses China objections to South China Sea mission

BEIJING: The US Navy on Tuesday dismissed Beijing's protests over a "freedom of navigation operation” conducted near a Chinese-held island in the South China Sea, in the latest incident drawing new attention to one of the world's potential military flashpoints. Hawaii's Mauna Loa: What hazards are posed by the world's largest active volcano?

HONOLULU: Lava is shooting 100 feet to 200 feet (30 to 60 meters) into the air as Hawaii's Mauna Loa,the world's largest active volcano, erupts for the first time in nearly 40 years.For now,lava is not threatening any homes or communities and no evacuation orders have been issued. Lava could eventually reach neighborhoods as it flows downhill though it could take a week or more for molten rock to reach populated areas. Mauna Loa is spewing sulfur dioxide and other volcanic gases.
